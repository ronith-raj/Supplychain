# RouteGuard AI - Hackathon Pitch Deck

### Slide 1: Title Slide
- **Title**: RouteGuard AI
- **Subtitle**: Climate-Resilient Smart Logistics
- **Visual**: High-quality image of a futuristic truck or a map with glowing nodes.
- **Hook**: "Supply chain disruptions cost businesses billions annually. What if we could see them coming?"

### Slide 2: The Problem
- **Problem**: Extreme weather events and sudden traffic disruptions cause massive delays in logistics.
- **Impact**: Spoiled goods, unhappy customers, and huge financial losses.
- **Current state**: Most systems are reactive, not proactive.

### Slide 3: The Solution (RouteGuard AI)
- **Solution**: A smart dashboard that integrates real-time mapping with an AI risk engine.
- **Key benefits**: 
  - Predicts and avoids risky routes before the truck departs.
  - Balances safety vs. speed with "Emergency" overrides.

### Slide 4: How It Works (Architecture)
- **Step 1**: User inputs origin and destination.
- **Step 2**: We fetch multiple route options via Google Maps API.
- **Step 3**: Our Risk Engine evaluates each route for climate and traffic threats.
- **Step 4**: The system recommends the safest route or triggers an Early Warning Modal.

### Slide 5: Live Demo
- **Action**: Switch to the live web app.
- **Flow**: Show the beautiful dashboard, do a normal safe route, then trigger the High-Risk modal to show the Early Warning System in action.
