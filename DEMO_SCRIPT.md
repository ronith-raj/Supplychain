# RouteGuard AI - 2 Minute Demo Script

**[0:00 - 0:15] Introduction (On Slide 1 & 2)**
"Hi judges, we are the creators of RouteGuard AI. Every year, climate events like unexpected floods or massive traffic jams cripple supply chains. We built RouteGuard AI to be a proactive, climate-resilient logistics dashboard."

**[0:15 - 0:45] The Interface & Normal Routing (Switch to Dashboard)**
"Here is our live prototype. As a logistics manager, I need to send a shipment from Chicago to Denver. Our dashboard uses a sleek glassmorphism UI to display critical metrics. 
Watch what happens when I calculate the route on 'Normal' priority. Behind the scenes, our engine generates multiple alternative routes and analyzes them for risk. It automatically selected the safest route, and updated our metrics."

**[0:45 - 1:15] The Early Warning System**
"But what if there's a major storm? Let me calculate a route from Miami to Houston. 
*(Click calculate until the red modal pops up)*
Boom. Our Early Warning System detected that even the safest route has an unacceptably high risk score. It blocks the shipment, pops up a warning, and down here in the sidebar, you can see exactly why: a Severe Flood Risk. As a manager, I can choose to cancel the shipment, or proceed anyway."

**[1:15 - 1:45] Emergency Routing**
"Finally, for medical supplies or urgent deliveries, we have 'Emergency' mode. If I switch to Emergency and hit calculate, the system bypasses the safety checks and instantly maps the absolute fastest physical route available."

**[1:45 - 2:00] Conclusion**
"RouteGuard AI takes supply chains from reactive to proactive. Thank you!"
